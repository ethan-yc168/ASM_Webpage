About the meeting

Event details

The Auditory Science Meeting (ASM) is an annual conference where hearing scientists from around the UK (and beyond) connect or reunite, and present their latest research. The meeting has existed in one form or another since 1977 and has previously been called the 'Basic Auditory Science (BAS) Meeting' and the 'BSA Short Papers Meeting'.

ASM2026 will take place in Manchester, and will be hosted jointly by Manchester Centre for Audiology and Deafness at The University of Manchester, and the University of Salford.

ASM2026 will include a variety of talks spanning basic to translational hearing research. Ample time will be scheduled for viewing posters and networking. Delegates may submit abstracts for either an oral presentation or a poster.

Further instructions for registering for this event or submitting an abstract will be posted to this page in due course.
